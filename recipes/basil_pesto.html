Basil Pesto Recipe

Description

This can be done quickly and easily using a blender

Ingredients

  • 3 cups chopped fresh basil
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1/8 cup Brazil nuts
  • 2/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder

Steps

  1. Place the basil in the blender
  2. Pour in 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  3. Blend basil into a paste
  4. Gradually add the pine nuts, Brazil nuts, Parmesan cheese, garlic, chili powder, and the remaining oil
  5. Continue to blend until smooth
