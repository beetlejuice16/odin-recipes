Best Fudgey Brownies

Description

This is the best brownies recipe I have ever made. With a sinful amount of butter and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 225 g good-quality chocolate, semi-sweet
  • 12 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 250 g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 95 g of flour
  • 30 g of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C.
  2. Line a 20 cm square baking dish with parchment paper.
  3. Chop chocolate into chunks and melt half of it.
  4. Mix butter, eggs, and sugar with an electric hand mixer. Beat in the eggs and vanilla for 1-2 minutes, until the mixture becomes fluffy and cool
  5. Whisk in the melted chocolate (not too hot to not cook the eggs), sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  6. Fold to incorporate the dry ingredients, careful not to overmix so they don't turn into cake.
  7. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then transfer to baking dish.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, depending on desired fudginess level.
